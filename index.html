Dr Semmelweiss and discovering of handwashing

A data visualization project about Dr. Ignaz Semmelweis and his discovery of the importance of handwashing could include information such as:
-The high rate of maternal deaths in the clinic and Dr. Semmelweis's observations and conclusions about the spread of infection.
-The introduction and implementation of handwashing as a means of preventing the spread of infection and the resulting decrease in maternal mortality rates.
This information could be presented in a variety of ways, such as graphs and charts showing the decrease in mortality rates after handwashing was introduced, timelines detailing the events of Dr. Semmelweis's life and discovery, or infographics comparing the death rates before and after the implementation of handwashing.
